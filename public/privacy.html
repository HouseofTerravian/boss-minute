Effective Date: March 9, 2026

The Boss Minute ("we," "us," or "our") is a premium newsletter and professional development service operated by House of Terravian / MCM Enterprises LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you subscribe to or use The Boss Minute.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address — when you subscribe, request a sample, or contact us
  • Work email or name — if provided during signup
  • Payment information — collected and processed by our third-party payment processor (Stripe); we do not store payment card data

Information collected automatically:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Pages visited and interaction data (via PostHog analytics)
  • UTM parameters and affiliate identifiers from URLs you arrive through
  • Browser locale (used to auto-detect your preferred currency)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver your daily Boss Minute newsletter
  • To process subscription payments and manage billing
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, account updates)
  • To improve our content, product experience, and conversion performance
  • To attribute subscription referrals to affiliates where applicable
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Analytics and Tracking

We use PostHog for product analytics. PostHog may collect anonymized event data including page views, button clicks, and A/B test variant assignments. This data is used to improve the product — it is not sold to third parties.

We also use browser localStorage to store your currency preference, A/B variant assignment, and affiliate attribution data. This data stays in your browser and is not transmitted to our servers independently.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • ConvertKit (Kit) — for email list management and newsletter delivery
  • PostHog — for product analytics
  • Legal authorities — if required by law or to protect platform integrity

5. Data Retention

Subscriber email addresses are retained while your subscription is active and for a reasonable period thereafter. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Attribution data stored in localStorage expires after 365 days.

6. Your Rights

To exercise these rights, contact: forbiddengnosis@mcmenterprisesllc.com

8. Children's Privacy
